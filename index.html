frequently asked questions

Got Questions?

Clear answers to help build absolute trust and align expectations before our collaboration.

How long does a typical project take? ▼

Landing pages and custom storefronts typically take between 2 to 3 weeks. Comprehensive full-stack platforms and complex WooCommerce subscription systems take around 4 to 6 weeks, depending on database size and API integrations.

Do you work internationally? ▼

Absolutely. Almost 90% of my collaborations are remote with clients based in the US, Europe, and the Middle East. I utilize asynchronous platforms (Slack, Loom, GitHub) to ensure clear, ongoing reporting across all standard timezones.

What technologies do you specialize in? ▼

My primary stack revolves around React.js, Next.js, and TypeScript on the frontend, and PHP and MySQL on the backend. For e-commerce and CMS setups, I build premium customized Liquid structures in Shopify, and ACF/PHP systems in WordPress and WooCommerce.

Do you redesign existing websites? ▼

Yes. I systematically audit existing setups to identify slow database calls, bloated plugins, and high checkout friction, completely rebuilding them from scratch into premium, search-friendly, fast-loading conversions.
